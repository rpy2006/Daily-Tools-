Our Hiring Process

We've designed our process to be transparent, respectful of your time, and focused on finding mutual fit.

1

Application Review

Our team reviews your application and will contact you within 1-2 weeks if there's a potential fit.

2

Initial Call

A 30-minute video call with a recruiter to discuss your background and the role in more detail.

3

Skills Assessment

A practical exercise or technical interview to assess your skills relevant to the position.

4

Team Interviews

Video interviews with 2-3 team members to discuss your experience and how you collaborate.

5

Offer

If it's a mutual fit, we extend an offer and welcome you to the DailyTools team!
