Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about DailyTools

Is DailyTools completely free to use?

Yes, DailyTools is completely free to use with no hidden costs or premium features. We believe productivity tools should be accessible to everyone.

How does DailyTools protect my privacy?

We prioritize your privacy by design. Most tools run entirely in your browser, meaning your data never leaves your device. We don't collect personal information or track your usage.

Can I use DailyTools offline?

Our Android app offers full offline functionality for all tools. The web version requires an internet connection to load, but some tools may work offline once loaded.

How often are new tools added?

We regularly add new tools based on user feedback and needs. Typically, we release 1-2 new tools every quarter. You can suggest new tools through our feedback form.

Do you offer custom solutions for businesses?

While our standard tools are free for everyone, we do offer custom solutions and white-label options for businesses. Contact our enterprise team at enterprise@dailytools.com for more information.
