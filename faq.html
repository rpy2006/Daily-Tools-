Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about DailyTools. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact our support team.

General Questions

What is DailyTools?

DailyTools is a free, privacy-focused platform offering a suite of online productivity tools. Our tools are designed to help you with everyday tasks without requiring sign-ups, subscriptions, or sharing personal data.

Is DailyTools completely free?

Yes, DailyTools is completely free to use with no hidden costs or premium features. We believe productivity tools should be accessible to everyone without financial barriers.

How does DailyTools make money?

DailyTools is currently funded by our founders and generous supporters who believe in our mission. We may explore ethical monetization options in the future that align with our values, such as optional donations or enterprise solutions, but the core tools will always remain free.

Account & Access

Do I need to create an account to use DailyTools?

No, you don't need to create an account to use most of our tools. We've designed DailyTools to be accessible immediately without any sign-up process. Simply visit our website and start using the tools you need.

How do I save my work without an account?

Most tools allow you to export or download your work directly to your device. For example, the notes app lets you download your notes as text files, and the password generator allows you to copy passwords to your clipboard. We recommend saving your work locally since we don't store any user data on our servers.

Tools & Features

What tools are currently available?

We currently offer several productivity tools including a calculator, music player, notes app, password generator, temporary email service, and timer/stopwatch. We're continuously working on adding new tools based on user feedback and needs.

How often are new tools added?

We typically add 1-2 new tools every quarter. Our development process is guided by user feedback and needs. You can suggest new tools through our contact form or community forums.

Can I use DailyTools offline?

Our Android app offers full offline functionality for all tools. The web version requires an internet connection to load, but some tools may work offline once loaded. We recommend using our Android app for the best offline experience.

Privacy & Security

How does DailyTools protect my privacy?

We prioritize your privacy by design. Most tools run entirely in your browser, meaning your data never leaves your device. We don't collect personal information, track your usage, or serve ads. Our temporary email service doesn't store emails longer than necessary, and our password generator works completely locally.

Does DailyTools use cookies?

We use minimal, essential cookies only for basic website functionality. We don't use tracking cookies or any form of analytics that would compromise your privacy. You can learn more in our Privacy Policy.

Is my data safe with DailyTools?

Since most tools operate locally in your browser, your data never reaches our servers. For tools that require server processing (like temporary email), we only store data temporarily and never share it with third parties. We follow security best practices to protect any data that does pass through our systems.

Technical Support

What browsers are supported?

DailyTools works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, and Opera. For the best experience, we recommend using the latest version of your browser. Some tools may have limited functionality on very old browsers.

Why isn't a tool working properly?

If you're experiencing issues with a tool, try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache. If the problem persists, it might be a temporary server issue or browser compatibility problem. Please contact our support team with details about the issue, your browser, and device.

Can I integrate DailyTools with other apps?

Currently, we don't offer official API integrations. However, many tools allow you to export data in standard formats that can be imported into other applications. If you have specific integration needs, please contact us with your suggestions.
